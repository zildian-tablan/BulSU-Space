BulSU Space · Academic Social Platform

User Manual & Onboarding Guide

Welcome to BulSU Space—the unified social, academic, and collaboration hub for students, faculty, alumni, and administrators of Bulacan State University. This guide walks you through secure sign-in, daily workflows, and the platform's real-time collaboration features.

Always in Sync

Live updates keep classes, orgs, and announcements aligned without needing to refresh.

Role-Aware

The experience adapts to whether you are a student, faculty member, alumni, or admin.

Secure by Design

Safeguards protect every conversation and keep campus information private.

1 · Onboarding Roadmap

Follow these steps as soon as your campus account is activated so your classmates and offices can find you.

Step 01 Set Up Your Account Check your outlook email for the automated account credentials sent by BulSU Space. If you did not receive it, contact the Campus Registrar Office.
Step 02 Log In Securely Visit https://bulsuspace.web.app/signin, and enter your credentials, choose Sign In. It is followed by the Multifactor Authentication step. If the page says your access is paused, wait for the notice or message the admin desk.
Step 03 Complete Your Profile Open Profile > Edit to add your preferred name, campus, program, and contact details so classmates know they are talking to you.
Step 04 Learn the Status Indicator Keep one tab open while you work so the online indicator stays accurate. Close the tab or sign out when you are finished.
  • Read the pinned announcement on the home feed for your college-specific reminders.
  • Join events or organization spaces recommended by your instructor or coordinator so you receive the right updates.
  • Bookmark support@bulsuspace.edu.ph so you can quickly request assistance if you get stuck.

2 · Main Navigation

The menu on the left (desktop) or bottom (mobile) is organized into the areas below. Open them in order during onboarding to understand what each space does.

Home Feed

See announcements, classroom reminders, club updates, and pinned posts from admins. Scroll daily to stay in sync.

Messages

Chat with classmates or staff. Presence indicators show whether the other person is active before you send a note.

Groups

Join academic or organization spaces. Each group has its own posts, files, and quick polls.

Events

Browse seminars, deadlines, and campus-wide programs. RSVP so organizers know you are attending.

Jobs & Opportunities

Alumni and partners can post openings. Students can save listings and message posters inside the same screen.

Profile & Settings

Update your photo, bio, and notification preferences. Use this area when something in your account changes.

Notifications

Tap the bell icon to review mentions, reactions, approvals, and reports that need your attention.

3 · Sharing Updates & Messaging

Use these quick steps whenever you want to post, reply, or reach someone directly.

Posting to the feed

  • Select the New Post button on the home feed.
  • Add your text. Use the attach icon to include images, documents, or short clips.
  • Choose the audience (campus, class group, or everyone) if the form offers the option.
  • Press Share. The post appears instantly for people who can view it.

Commenting and reactions

  • Open any post and type your reply in the comment field.
  • Use reactions to acknowledge updates without writing a full response.
  • If something seems off-topic or unsafe, choose the Report option in the post menu so admins can review it.

Sending messages

  • Go to Messages and pick an existing conversation or start a new one.
  • The status dot beside each name shows if the person is online or recently active.
  • Send text, photos, or attachments. Messages sync across web sessions as long as you stay signed in.

Joining or creating groups

  • Open Groups, search for your class, organization, or department, then tap Join.
  • Group moderators can approve membership requests and remove old threads when needed.
  • Only create a new group if your college or adviser asked you to manage it.

5 · Typical Day in BulSU Space

Start of Day

Open the home feed, skim pinned announcements, and acknowledge any alerts waiting in your notifications.

Midday Collaboration

Share updates, answer classmates, and upload files to a group post so everyone has the same copy.

Afternoon Follow-ups

Check Messages to respond to pending chats, confirm meeting times, or send reminders.

Evening Wrap-up

Review the Events list for tomorrow, log out of shared devices, and close the tab once your status can go offline.

6 · Safety & Support

Your good habits

  • Keep your password private and change it if you suspect someone else has seen it.
  • When using a shared computer, sign out and close the tab so no one can post as you.
  • Use the "Report" button beneath any post or message that feels unsafe or disrespectful.

What the platform handles

  • Only the correct roles (student, faculty, alumni, admin) can view or edit each space.
  • Unusual sign-in patterns trigger temporary locks until support reviews them.
  • Daily activity records help admins trace issues and keep the community accountable.

7 · Troubleshooting & Contacts

Signed in but the feed looks empty

Refresh the page, then check if your profile shows the right campus and role. If not, message your college coordinator to update it.

Status stays offline even when I'm active

Keep only one BulSU Space tab open and interact with the page every few minutes. Battery saver modes can pause the status indicator.

Uploads take too long

Compress large photos or videos before sending. A stable Wi-Fi connection shortens upload time for everyone.

I was locked out after mistakes

Wait for the cooldown timer shown on the sign-in page. If you need urgent access, email the admin desk with your ID number.

Notifications feel overwhelming

Open Profile > Settings and lower the alerts you don’t need, then leave only the essentials such as mentions and approvals.

A post needs correction after publishing

Use the three-dot menu on the post to edit the text or remove it. Repost only after updating the files or wording.

I can’t find a required group

Search by course code or organization name inside the Groups tab. If it still doesn’t appear, ask your adviser to invite you directly.

Someone posted something inappropriate

Use the Report option under the post or comment. Describe what happened so moderators can act quickly.

I switched devices and my messages look empty

Sign out, refresh the browser, then sign back in so the app rebuilds your message list from the server.

Calendar reminders aren’t appearing

Open Events, select the entry, and tap “Add to calendar” again. Confirm your browser allows pop-ups so the reminder can sync.
